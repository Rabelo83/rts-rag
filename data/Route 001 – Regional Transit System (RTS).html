Route 1
Rosa Parks to Butler Plaza

New alignment begins August 18. Servicing Center Drive. See new map below. Changes outlined in red.

  • M-F: Every 18-36 min. First run BPTS 6:27am / Last run BPTS 10:39pm
  • Sat: Every 36 min. First run RPDS 7:06am / Last run BPTS 6:27pm
  • Sun: Every 36 min. First run RPDS 10:06am / Last run BPTS 5:15pm
  • “R” next to trips on the Weekday schedule means that trip will operate on Reduced Service Days.
  • Weekend schedule runs during reduced service.
  • These directions are for planning purposes only. You may find that construction projects, traffic, weather, or other events may cause conditions to differ from the map results, and you should plan your route accordingly. You must obey all signs or notices regarding your route
